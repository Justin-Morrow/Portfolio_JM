About Me


Justin Morrow lives in Seattle and he is an employee of Merrill Lynch Wealth Management. His focus areas have included program management of sales, operations, financial planning and client experience.

Justin is an elected member of Merrill Lynch Wealth Management's Client Associate Growth Network, which develops strategies to enhance the partnership of Client Associates and Wealth Management Advisors across Merrill Lynch Wealth Management by developing and delivering programs on growth and operational efficiency.

Justin has received the Merrill Lynch Wealth Management Top 100 Responsible Service Growth Award each year from 2017 through 2021, which is given to associates upholding Merrill Lynch Wealth Management's standards of excellence.

Before joining Merrill Lynch Wealth Management, he was at Capital One Investing and worked in multiple roles within cross-functional teams, including sales & service, training, product, marketing, legal and compliance.
