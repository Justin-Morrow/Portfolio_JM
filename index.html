About Me

Justin works in financial services. He focuses on assisting clients in the development of financial strategies and provides client support services. Justin holds an undergraduate degree in journalism from Western Washington University, a master’s in sport business leadership from the Albers School of Business & Economics at Seattle University, maintains the CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ certification and Chartered Retirement Planning Counselor designation along with the Series 4, 7, 24 and 66 FINRA registrations.
